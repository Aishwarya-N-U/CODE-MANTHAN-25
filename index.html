About CODE MANTHAN'25

CODE MANTHAN'25 is more than just a hackathon – it's a platform where creative minds come together to solve real-world problems through innovative technology solutions. Join us for an intensive 2-day coding marathon that challenges you to think beyond conventional boundaries.

Who Can Participate?

  • Students from all engineering disciplines
  • Teams of exactly 4 members
  • Maximum 40 teams will be selected
  • Open to all skill levels – beginners welcome!

What You'll Gain

  • Industry mentorship and guidance
  • Networking with like-minded innovators
  • Hands-on experience with cutting-edge APIs
  • Certificates and exciting prizes

Workshop & Mentoring

A mandatory workshop on September 19, 2025 will cover problem statements, judging criteria, and Gemini API demos. Shortlisted teams will then undergo 2 weeks of mentorship from industry experts.
